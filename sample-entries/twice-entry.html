THIS IS FOR — TWICE

Feeling: Empowered

After two whole months of procrastinating over a class project, I finally got around to finishing! It felt so invigorating to click the Submit button when I did, I almost forgot all about the feelings of dread that clouded my thoughts countless days beforehand. Looking past that, I just felt like this song was the perfect way to celebrate the hard work and dedication that I put into my project. Not to mention, my final product was not too far off from what I originally wanted—so consider me satisfied!
