November 20, 2025

Fa La La — Justin Bieber (ft. Boyz II Men)

Feeling: Festive

With two major holidays coming up, tonight felt like the perfect night to help my mom get the house prepared for all of the holiday festivities! Of course, I had to play my favorite Christmas album to make putting up decorations that more exciting. Bonding with family over something as simple as decorating the house really makes my heart happy.

Everything about the holidays just brings me so much joy and when we combine this with Justin Bieber’s Under the Mistletoe album, it’s hard to believe that some people’s favorite holiday isn’t Christmas!
