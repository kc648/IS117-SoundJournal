November 10, 2025

Turning Green — Maki

Feeling: Content

Today, I felt like I was the main character of an indie, coming-of-age romance movie. It was the first time I had seen a significant person from my past and I was overcome with feelings of regret and nostalgia. Maki’s song perfectly captures the essence of what I felt tonight. He lists the nagging questions that were somehow always at the back of my mind and were now at its forefront.

  • How have they been doing since we stopped talking?
  • Did they miss me like I missed them?
  • Can we ever go back to being friends?
