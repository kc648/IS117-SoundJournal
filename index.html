WELCOME TO SOUND JOURNAL!

A place where music turns memories into stories we can hear again.

SoundJournal is a personal journal archive where you can log special moments in your life. Similar to videos and images, music can also hold memories. SoundJournal capitalizes on the emotions and emotional associations we make with the songs that we listen to when we go certain situations in life. Utilize your personal journal to curate your life soundtrack and reminisce on what your year consisted of--musically and emotionally.
